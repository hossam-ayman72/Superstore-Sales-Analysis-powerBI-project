# Superstore-Sales-Analysis-powerBI-project

# Project Overview
The goal of this project is to provide a holistic view of the business performance by tracking key metrics (KPIs) and identifying trends across various dimensions like geography, product categories, and customer segments.

# Key Features
Executive Summary: High-level KPIs including Total Sales, Total Profit, Quantity Sold, and Profit Margin.

Sales Trend Analysis: Time-series analysis to identify seasonality and growth patterns (Yearly/Quarterly/Monthly).

Geographic Insights: Interactive map visuals showing performance across different Regions and States.

Product & Category Performance: Deep dive into which categories and sub-categories are driving revenue vs. those impacting margins.

Customer Segmentation: Analysis of sales distribution among Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.

Interactive Slicers: Dynamic filtering by Date, Region, Category, and Shipping Mode for personalized data exploration


# Tech Stack & Tools
Power BI Desktop: The core tool for data modeling and visualization.

Power Query: Used for Data Cleaning, Transformation (ETL), and shaping the dataset.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Developed complex measures and calculated columns for advanced business logic.

Data Source: Superstore Sales Dataset (Excel/CSV).

# Key Insights

Profitability Analysis 
• Technology dominates profit with 50.6%, making it the core business driver 
• Office Supplies provide steady profitability at 42.7% 
• Furniture underperforms at 6.4%, indicating need for strategic review 
• Product-level variance shows significant opportunity for optimization 
Market Segmentation 
• Consumer segment represents largest contributor with 1.2 M volume 
• Corporate segment shows substantial volume at 0.7M 
• Home Office remains underdeveloped but offers growth potential
